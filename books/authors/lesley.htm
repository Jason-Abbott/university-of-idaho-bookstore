Guest Author

Craig Lesley

Thanks to everyone who attended the Craig Lesley reading of The Sky Fisherman on September 12th. Craig is a very lively and entertaining speaker and The Sky Fisherman is a fantastic book. The event was a great success.

We still have a few signed copies of the The Sky Fisherman in stock. As Shawn Vidmar wrote in her review which appeared in the Argonaut on September 10, "it cleverly combines a human story with nature, art, innocence, scandal and life. For those with a love of rivers, either rafting or fishing, this is a must read. For others who enjoy a great, energetic story, this is also the book for you." Pick up your copy while they last.
